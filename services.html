Our Services

We deliver structured quality and regulatory capabilities that enable medical device organizations to operate effectively within regulated environments.

Quality Systems as an Operating Capability

Our engagements focus on embedding regulatory requirements into operating models that support execution discipline, inspection readiness, and long-term system stability.

1

Quality Management System Enablement

Support for the establishment and sustainment of ISO 13485 and MDSAP-aligned quality management systems structured to operate as part of the organization's core operating model.

The emphasis is on system simplification, logical process sequencing, and alignment between documented controls and operational reality.
Capabilities
  • Quality system architecture and governance
  • ISO 13485 and MDSAP alignment
  • Internal audit and inspection readiness frameworks
  • CAPA, risk management, and post-market system integration
2

Managed Quality System Services

Ongoing operational ownership of client quality management systems over defined engagement periods.

This engagement model enables continuous compliance, documentation integrity, and sustained inspection readiness without dependence on ad-hoc interventions.
Scope
  • QMS maintenance and controlled updates
  • Documentation change management
  • CAPA and audits support
  • Continuous alignment with applicable regulations
3

EU MDR Compliance Alignment

Structured preparation, alignment, and lifecycle maintenance of EU MDR technical documentation and post-market processes, including transition from MDD to MDR.

Engagements focus on remediation, documentation updates, and lifecycle maintenance of existing EU MDR technical files.
Capabilities
  • Technical documentation
  • Clinical evaluation and risk documentation preparation & updates
  • Post-market surveillance and vigilance system alignment
  • Ongoing EU MDR compliance support
4

Indian Regulatory Compliance (CDSCO)

Quality system and documentation alignment supporting compliance with Indian regulatory requirements for medical devices.

Support is tailored to Indian regulatory requirements, covering documentation alignment and post-approval compliance obligations.
Capabilities
  • Quality system alignment for Indian regulatory expectations
  • Documentation support for CDSCO submissions
  • Post-approval compliance and lifecycle support
5

AI & Digital Quality System Governance

Establishment of ISO 42001-aligned management systems integrated with existing medical device quality frameworks.

Services focus on governance structures, management system alignment, and lifecycle controls rather than regulatory submission ownership.
Capabilities
  • ISO 42001 management system implementation
  • AI governance and risk framework alignment
  • Integration with ISO 13485 and MDSAP systems
