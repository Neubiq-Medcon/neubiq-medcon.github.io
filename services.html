Our Services

Our services are designed to support medical device organizations in establishing, operating, and sustaining quality systems within regulated environments.

Structured Quality & Regulatory Support

Engagements emphasize operational usability, inspection readiness, and long-term system effectiveness.

1

Quality Management System Enablement

We support medical device organizations in establishing and sustaining ISO 13485 and MDSAP-aligned quality management systems designed to function as part of day-to-day operations, not as static compliance documentation.

The focus is on simplifying system structure, sequencing processes logically, and aligning procedures with actual organizational workflows.
Capabilities
  • Quality system architecture and governance
  • ISO 13485 and MDSAP alignment
  • Internal audit and inspection readiness frameworks
  • CAPA, risk management, and post-market system integration
2

Managed Quality System Services

We assume ongoing responsibility for the operation and maintenance of client quality management systems over defined engagement periods.

This model supports continuous compliance, documentation integrity, and sustained inspection readiness without reliance on ad-hoc consulting.
Scope
  • QMS maintenance and controlled updates
  • Documentation change and revision management
  • CAPA and audit follow-up support
  • Continuous alignment with applicable regulations
3

EU MDR Compliance Alignment

Support for EU MDR compliance through structured alignment and maintenance of technical documentation and post-market processes.

Engagements focus on remediation, documentation updates, and lifecycle maintenance of existing EU MDR technical files.
Capabilities
  • Technical documentation and DHF alignment
  • Clinical evaluation and risk documentation updates
  • Post-market surveillance and vigilance system alignment
  • Ongoing EU MDR compliance support
4

Indian Regulatory Compliance (CDSCO)

Quality system and documentation alignment supporting compliance with Indian regulatory requirements for medical devices.

We understand India's unique regulatory landscape and provide targeted support for CDSCO submissions and post-approval compliance.
Capabilities
  • Quality system alignment for Indian regulatory expectations
  • Documentation support for CDSCO submissions
  • Post-approval compliance and lifecycle support
5

AI & Digital Quality System Governance

Establishment of ISO 42001-aligned management systems integrated with existing medical device quality frameworks.

Services focus on governance structures, management system alignment, and lifecycle controls rather than regulatory submission ownership.
Capabilities
  • ISO 42001 management system implementation
  • AI governance and risk framework alignment
  • Integration with ISO 13485 and MDSAP systems
