Our 4-Phase Methodology

A structured roadmap that transforms your current state into an audit-ready, scalable QMS aligned with FDA, EU MDR, and India CDSCO requirements.

1

Assessment

We evaluate your current Quality Management System against applicable regulatory frameworks (ISO 13485, MDSAP, QMSR) and your specific product type.

What we review: Existing documentation, processes, organizational structure, compliance gaps, and regulatory risk areas.

Your Output:

Comprehensive gap analysis, prioritized risk areas, and a clear roadmap of improvement actions ranked by regulatory impact

2

Planning

Based on assessment findings, we design a tailored QMS roadmap aligned to your product lifecycle, organization maturity, and target markets.

What we define: Implementation phases, responsibility matrix, resource needs, timelines, compliance milestones, and success metrics.

Your Output:

Detailed implementation plan, documentation structure, process flowcharts, and a realistic timeline with clear go/no-go decision points

3

Implementation

Hands-on deployment of QMS processes, procedures, and controls. We work directly with your team to embed regulatory thinking into daily operations.

What we do: Documentation development, process training, system deployment, change management, and integration with R&D and software teams.

Your Output:

Live, operational QMS with trained personnel, controlled documentation, effective processes, and integration into your product development cycle

4

Validation & Readiness

We verify system effectiveness through internal audits, management reviews, and readiness assessments. For AI devices, we validate model governance and monitoring controls.

What we verify: System performance, compliance evidence, audit readiness, submission preparedness, and inspector confidence.

Your Output:

Audit-ready QMS with objective evidence, submission-ready documentation, and your team fully confident in regulatory readiness
