Our Approach

We follow a structured, risk-based methodology to design and implement Quality Management Systems that are compliant, scalable, and audit-ready. Our approach aligns with ISO 13485, MDSAP, QMSR, and integrates AI lifecycle controls where applicable.

1. Assessment

We begin by evaluating the current state of your Quality Management System against applicable regulatory and standard requirements. This includes reviewing existing documentation, processes, records, and organizational readiness.

Output: Gap analysis, risk areas, and prioritized improvement actions.

2. Planning

Based on assessment outcomes, we design a tailored QMS roadmap aligned to your product type, organizational maturity, and market strategy. Responsibilities, timelines, and compliance objectives are clearly defined.

Output: QMS implementation plan, documentation structure, and milestones.

3. Implementation

We support hands-on deployment of QMS processes, procedures, and records. This includes documentation development, training, process rollout, and integration into day-to-day operations.

Output: Operational QMS with trained personnel and controlled documentation.

4. Validation & Readiness

We verify system effectiveness through internal audits, management review support, CAPA implementation, and audit readiness activities. For AI-enabled devices, model validation evidence and monitoring controls are reviewed.

Output: Audit-ready QMS supported by objective evidence.
