About NeubiQ MedCon Services

NeubiQ MedCon Services is a specialized consulting firm focused exclusively on Quality Management System (QMS) compliance for medical device manufacturers. We support organizations across the full lifecycle — from early-stage startups to established manufacturers expanding into global markets.

EXPERTISE ALIGNED WITH GLOBAL STANDARDS

Our Focus

We work solely within the medical device domain, delivering QMS solutions aligned with ISO 13485, MDSAP, and QMSR requirements. This exclusive focus enables deep regulatory understanding and practical implementation.

What We Deliver

Our consulting approach emphasizes audit-ready systems, not just documentation. We design QMS frameworks that integrate seamlessly into daily operations and stand up to regulatory scrutiny.

AI-Enabled Medical Devices

Where applicable, we support QMS integration for AI-enabled medical devices by aligning AI lifecycle controls — including data management, model validation, change control, and monitoring — with ISO/IEC 42001 and ISO 13485 requirements.

Our Philosophy

We believe compliance should be structured, efficient, and sustainable. Our work is guided by regulatory intent, risk-based thinking, and long-term system effectiveness rather than short-term certification goals.
